Restore instructions:
1) To restore this snapshot, copy the files from this folder to the workspace root (F:\\PORTFOLIO), overwriting existing files:
   - portfolio.html
   - style.css
   - slider.js

2) Confirm assets (images) are present in `portifolio_home_pictures/` and `images/`.

3) Optionally create a backup of the current workspace before restoring.

Example (PowerShell):
cp .\portfolio.html ..\..
cp .\style.css ..\..
cp .\slider.js ..\..\

(These commands assume you `cd` into the snapshot folder first.)
